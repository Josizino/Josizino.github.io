01

Headline Highest Impact

Shows up in every search result, message, and comment. This is your hook.

headline · 220 chars max
Cybersecurity & Data Analytics | Blue Team · Threat Detection · Secure Dev | NSU '26 | Open to Cybersecurity & IT Roles
🎯

Why this works

Front-loads the two fields recruiters search for. The pipe separators make it scannable in 2 seconds. Closing with "Open to…" signals availability without sounding desperate.

🚫

What to avoid

"Student at NSU" — wastes prime real estate. "Aspiring…" — undermines confidence. Lead with what you do, not what you hope to become.

06

Profile Quick Wins Do These Today

Settings and structural changes that boost visibility with zero writing required.

📸

Profile Photo

Professional headshot with a clean background. LinkedIn profiles with photos get 21× more views. Wear what you'd wear to an interview.

🖼️

Banner / Cover Image

Use a dark cybersecurity-themed banner — a terminal screenshot, a network map, or a custom graphic with your name and tagline. Free: Canva.

🔗

Custom URL

Set it to linkedin.com/in/jose-matos-cyber or similar. Looks professional on resumes and email signatures.

🟢

"Open to Work" Frame

Enable it — visible to recruiters only if you prefer. Add roles: Cybersecurity Analyst, SOC Analyst, IT Security Specialist, Anti-Cheat Engineer.

⭐

Featured Section

Add your portfolio link (josematos.dev) and resume PDF as Featured items. This puts them above the fold on your profile.

🎓

NSU & Activities

Add NCAE Cyber Games as an Activity / Extracurricular under your NSU education entry. Recruiters scan education for clubs and competitions.

✍️

Post Weekly

Share one short post per week — a CTF you completed, a tool you learned, a security news take. Consistent activity multiplies profile views.

🤝

Ask for Endorsements

Message 3–5 classmates or colleagues and swap skill endorsements. Prioritize: Python, Cybersecurity, Splunk, Linux.
